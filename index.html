About Us

IT Nation is students one stop destination for their needs regarding IT & CS. Our website provides all the kind of help you may need in your IT & CS journey including roadmaps, resources (courses,videos,books etc), upcoming workshops and webinars, job openings and personalized mentorship. All the features in our website are designed keeping in mind the needs of the students working or aspire to work in the IT & CS industry.
